Every year, we celebrate Subaru Loves Pets month by helping Underdogs find loving homes.
At Subaru, we have a long-standing commitment to loving pets. That is why we have partnered
with local animal welfare organizations to help over 230,000 pets in need across the country.
We have also celebrated Subaru Loves Pets month by establishing a nationwide effort to help
shelter dogs find loving homes with special emphasis on the Underdogs with special needs.
In addition, we have joined hands with local animal shelters to help with the adoption of
thousands of shelter pets around the country.
